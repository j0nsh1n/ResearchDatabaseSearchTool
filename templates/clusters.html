Generate Clusters

Clustering groups your articles by topic using their embeddings - create embeddings on the Data Management page first (that job can finish in the background). Density (default) finds natural topics itself and parks odd papers in an “outliers” group - you do not choose a count. Prefer a fixed number of groups? Switch method to K-Means or Hierarchical, then use Auto (app picks the cleanest count) or uncheck Auto and set the slider yourself. Re-clustering replaces groups but keeps exclusions (and your screening report will still reflect them).

Density mode chooses how many clusters automatically. To turn Auto off and set a number by hand, switch Method to K-Means or Hierarchical above.
