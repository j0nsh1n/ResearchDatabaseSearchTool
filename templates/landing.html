Multi-source search

Pull articles for any topic from 12 databases at once — PubMed, Europe PMC, arXiv, OpenAlex, Semantic Scholar, ClinicalTrials.gov, CORE and more — all fetched in parallel.

🧠

Semantic embeddings

Every article is turned into a meaning-based vector (GPU-accelerated when available), so papers are matched by concept, not just keywords.

🧩

Clustering & triage

Group your papers into themed clusters with distinct auto-generated labels, then screen out whole off-topic clusters — or single papers — in one click.

🔄

Duplicate resolution

Find near-identical papers across sources, compare them side by side, and auto-keep the best copy of each — the redundant ones drop out of your results.

🎯

Similarity search

Describe your study in plain text or PICO format and rank the most relevant papers in your collection. Export results to CSV or text.

🔒

Private workspace

Each account gets its own isolated database — your fetched articles, embeddings, and searches stay entirely your own.
